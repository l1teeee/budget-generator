# Product

## Register

product

## Users

BudgetFlow AI is for freelancers, agencies, studios, and small service teams who need to turn client/project context into a polished quote without building a spreadsheet. Users are in a task-focused workflow: capture client details, define scope, pick services, review totals, and export a client-ready document.

## Product Purpose

The product creates editable local quotes and budgets with a guided intake, live A4 preview, PDF export, JSON import/export, brand settings, and local persistence. Success means a user can start from manual entry, JSON, or rough project context, resolve missing information quickly, and leave with a clean quote ready to send.

## Brand Personality

Premium, editorial, precise. The interface should feel like a trustworthy financial document tool with the visual confidence of the existing BudgetFlow landing page: dark ink, cream paper, blue accents, restrained motion, and concise guidance.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS gradients, dashboard clutter, over-decorated cards, fake AI claims, cloud-only assumptions, spreadsheet-like intimidation, and flows that hide the A4 preview. Do not imply backend features, login, payments, real AI generation, remote history, or Excel export unless those are implemented.

## Design Principles

1. Keep the user moving from incomplete context to export-ready quote.
2. Make missing information visible, specific, and easy to answer.
3. Preserve the landing page identity while keeping the generator practical and calm.
4. Treat AI as an adapter-ready workflow, not a fake promise.
5. Keep all local-first guarantees obvious and intact.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for text and controls. Preserve keyboard-accessible form controls, clear focus states, visible validation, and `prefers-reduced-motion` support. Motion should communicate state and never block task completion.
